Hi, I'm Sara.

I am a digital product designer with a solid background in user-centered design principles. I have a bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Michigan supplemented with a User Experience & User Interface Design apprenticeship from Bloc. I began my journey into the UX/UI design world when I discovered that this was the place where my creative and analytical talents could work together in harmony. My previous work experiences in research and data analytics have prepared me well to design solutions backed by data. My goal as a designer is to design solutions that reach the masses and help ease their daily lives.

My general process when approaching a design challenge:

  1. Research - gather information for a deeper understanding of the project. This may include speaking to clients/stakeholders, conducting user research in the form of surveys/interviews, usability testing and competitive analysis.
  2. Plan - use the data gathered above to develop an insight and make informed design decisions. This includes creating user personas, user stories, user flows, and wireframes.
  3. Build - using the information above, create a workable prototype that can be tested.
  4. Test - conduct usability testing on the prototype and iterate on the design until goals are successfully met.

When I am not perfecting my craft, you might find me learning classical Arabic, practicing photography, reading the latest news in design and tech or secretly observing people as I pretend to space out. Along with being a tireless problem-solver, I am a hopeless chocoholic, an empathetic over-thinker and a spiritual traveler.
